Attendance

If you are unable to make it during our normal league play, you can either pre-golf up to one week in advance or before the next week's normal league play. Note that the attendance points are different if you make-up your round.

Matchups

Each player will play all other league players once and only once. The matchups for each week have been randomly selected with consideration for playing each player only once. If a player is absent that week and does not make it up during the week, there is no makeup for the matchup.
